About Me

Who is Seetharam Yellaboina?

Seetharam Yellaboina, also known as Seetha, is a psychological thriller and crime author. In 2023, at the age of 19, he published the novella Unbound Justice: The Cyber Chronicle and writes short stories, poems, and novellas that explore morality, suspense, and the darker corners of the human mind. His work blurs the lines between right and wrong, delving into complex emotions and choices, designed to grip, provoke thought, and linger long after the last page.
